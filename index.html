How to Use AuraVT

  • 1

    Click Start Tracking and allow camera access. The AI takes ~5 seconds to load.

  • 2

    Step Back: Ensure your webcam can see your face, shoulders, and hands for full-body tracking.

  • 3

    Upload a custom Avatar Texture (like a smiley face or anime eyes) to map onto the 3D model.

  • 4

    Streaming to OBS: Set Chroma Key to "Transparent". In OBS, add a Browser Source, paste this URL, and check "Route audio via OBS".
